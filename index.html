About

I'm a Software Quality Engineering Expert with 14+ years of experience in the software industry, specializing in software development and Quality Engineering. Throughout my career, I have focused on building quality into software from the earliest stages of development, helping organizations deliver reliable, scalable, and production-ready applications.

My expertise spans technical quality engineering, including API and backend testing, database validation, performance testing, test automation architecture, CI/CD-integrated quality practices, and risk-based testing. I also have extensive experience in functional testing and business domain validation across enterprise-scale systems, ensuring that software not only meets technical standards but also fulfills business objectives.

With a strong software development background, I approach testing from an engineering perspective. I analyze system architecture, identify quality risks early, diagnose the root causes of defects, and design effective testing strategies that improve software reliability while reducing delivery risk. I believe quality should be engineered throughout the Software Development Lifecycle rather than inspected at the end.

I enjoy solving complex quality challenges, optimizing testing approaches, and continuously learning new technologies and engineering practices that help organizations build better software with greater confidence.
